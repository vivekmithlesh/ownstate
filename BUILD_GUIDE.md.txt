## CORE IDEA (never lose sight of this)
OwnState lets anyone **buy, sell, rent, or lease ANY property on Earth** ‚Äî from a village
plot to a flat, a mansion, or a private island ‚Äî entirely online, no agent needed.
Its signature feature is **Digital Land Fencing**: mark your land's boundary on a map,
link your government papers, and protect it forever.

## CORE FEATURES (all must exist by the end)
1. Live property search on an interactive map with real market rates
2. No physical visit needed ‚Äî full online discovery
3. List your land/property to reach buyers & renters
4. Digital Land Fencing ‚Äî draw boundary on map, save to PostGIS, link documents
5. Personal dashboard ‚Äî properties, listings, saved, enquiries, deals
6. Rent / sell / lease flats, houses, commercial, plots
7. Legal transaction pipeline (Deal Room) ‚Äî token, agreement, payment
8. Every property type: flat, house, villa, commercial, penthouse, mansion, chateau, island
9. Lease support
10. Cinematic 3D Earth hero on the homepage that tells the story visually

# BRICK 01 ‚Äî Database Schema (PostGIS)

**USER ACTION:** Supabase ‚Üí Database ‚Üí Extensions ‚Üí enable **postgis**.

**Claude Code ‚Äî create `supabase/schema.sql`** (full Postgres + PostGIS):
